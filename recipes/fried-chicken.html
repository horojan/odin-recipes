Fried Chicken

Description

This fried chicken batter yields the crispiest, spiciest, homemade fried chicken I have ever tasted! It is equally good served hot or cold and has been a picnic favorite in my family for years.

Ingredients

  • One (1) of quart vegetable oil for frying
  • Four (4) of cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • One (1) ½ tablespoons of garlic salt
  • One (1) tablespoon of ground black pepper
  • One (1) tablespoon of paprika
  • ½ teaspoon of poultry seasoning
  • One (1) ½ cups of beer, or as needed
  • Two (2) egg yolks, beaten
  • One (1) teaspoon of salt
  • ¼ teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • One (1) whole chicken, cut into pieces
